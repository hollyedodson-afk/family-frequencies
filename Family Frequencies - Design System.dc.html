The mark & lockups

A gold sun disc over a navy rolling-wave stroke — the daybreak swell. Sun over a swell: coastal, daytime, and the wave still oscillates like a frequency. The stroke width of 4 (at the 48 viewbox) is the locked proportion — scale the whole SVG, never restroke.

Type · Barlow Condensed

One typeface for the whole brand. Headlines run heavy (800–900), uppercase, tight tracking and line-height. Accent words inside a headline go rose. Body is weight 500. Eyebrow labels are tiny, wide-tracked uppercase.
